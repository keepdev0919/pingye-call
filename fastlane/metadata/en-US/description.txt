Boring meeting, awkward dinner, moment you need to leave — Pingye Call gives you a natural exit.

Schedule a fake incoming call for whenever you need it. A few taps and the call arrives right on time.

【Features】
• Timer scheduling: 1, 5, 10, 30 minutes — or set any custom time
• Custom caller name: the call shows whoever you choose
• Instant trigger: tap the screen anytime to call immediately

【Privacy】
No data collected. The caller name you enter stays on your device only.
